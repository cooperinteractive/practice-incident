First response communication model

Say less, say it clearly, and say what happens next.

The goal is not to fill the room with updates. The goal is to make every update useful: what we know, what we are checking, who is affected, what the team is doing now, and when the next message is due.

Confirm before amplifying Separate observed facts from assumptions, rumors, and pending analysis.
Name the audience Internal responders, leaders, customers, partners, and regulators need different levels of detail.
Use a clock Every update should include a concrete next-update time or a reason one cannot be set.
Close the loop Capture decisions, owners, and follow-ups so the after-action recap is not guesswork.
